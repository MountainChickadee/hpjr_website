About High Plains Junk Removal

Locally owned. Woman-owned. Wyoming strong

High Plains Junk Removal was built on a simple idea: show up when you say you will, treat people fairly, and do the job right.

We’re a woman-owned, locally operated junk removal business based in Casper, Wyoming, serving homeowners, renters, landlords, and businesses throughout the surrounding area. We provide full-service junk removal — meaning we handle all the lifting, loading, hauling, and proper disposal so you don’t have to.

Whether you’re clearing out a garage, dealing with a move-out, or just tired of staring at unwanted clutter, our goal is to make the process straightforward and stress-free.

What you can expect

When you work with High Plains Junk Removal, you can expect:

  • Clear communication from start to finish
  • Honest, upfront quotes before any work begins
  • Respect for your property and belongings
  • Reliable service from someone who lives and works in this community

We don’t cut corners, and we don’t believe in hidden fees. If we say we’ll be there, we’ll be there.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple help people take back their space — without stress, confusion, or hassle.

We’re not here to upsell you or rush you. We’re here to do solid work, charge fairly, and leave you with a space that feels lighter and more usable than when we arrived.

We’re not just hauling junk.

We’re helping you move forward.
