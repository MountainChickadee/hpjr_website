How Pricing Works

Every junk removal job is different, and we believe in pricing that’s fair and transparent. Rather than posting “one-size-fits-all” prices, we provide on-site estimates so you only pay for what you need.

What Determines Your Quote?

Your estimate is based on:

  • Volume of material
  • Weight and type of materials
  • Distance to loading area
  • Stairs, tight spaces, or obstacles
  • Special handling (freon, tires, electronics)
  • Travel distance

Our Promise

You will always know the price before we begin.

  • No hidden fees
  • No pressure
  • No suprise invoices

Why On-Site Quotes Are Better

Every job is unique — and an in-person estimate ensures:

  • Accuracy
  • Fair pricing
  • No misunderstandings
  • No "one more item" drama
  • No weight surprises at the landfill
